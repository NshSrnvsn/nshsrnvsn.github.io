My friend's mom got me a Banana Chai Latte from Foxtale Coffee. It was, let's say, an interesting combo. The taste wasn't so bad, it had cinnamon cream on top, tasted like chai except ** drum rolls ** too sweet for my liking. But I've got a low tolerance for sweetness, so it's a given that I'd dislike this. I don't see it as a drawback per se, just a personal preference. The drink isn't hot, it's room temperature, again, interesting. 

Now I have to wonder who woke up one day and thought, "You know what chai needs? Banana!". And now here we are. Living in a world where banana chai exists. The audacity to ruin chai. The creativity. The chaos.

Would I drink it again? No. Did I drink the whole thing? No. 
How much did I drink, you ask? Enough sips to take a picture, write a rant review and post it.
